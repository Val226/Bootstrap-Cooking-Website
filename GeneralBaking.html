Let's Learn to Bake!

Baking is an art, some would say it is a difficult art; Others don't agree. Our goal is to make the baking experience fun and stress-free

You'll find there are many levels to cooking, and that is because things like sourdough bread takes more knowledge and practice than cookies. Our class levels are broken into different groups because of this, but they are reccomendations more than anything.

Something to keep in mind: We will do our best to accommodate dietary restrictions, but we are not perfect. Please let your instructor know of any dietary restrictions you are working with.

Cookies & Bars (Level 1)

Cookies & Bars are fun to learn and a great place to start if you're looking to get into baking. These are also great things to bring to your next potluck or party! Here we'll begin talking about the different ingredients and how they effect your recipes

Cakes & Cupcakes (Level 2)

Some find cupcakes easier than cakes, some see it the other way around. Either way, you'll learn the different types of flours you can use as well as go more indepth on the ingredients you'll use and their effects on your recipes.

Breads (Not sourdough) (Level 3)

Breads are fun! Some call it annoying and stressful, but at the end of the day it is so rewarding to be able to show off your loaf of bread. Here we will discuss the different types of breads, the flour options and the usual ingredients.

Sourdough everything! (Level 4)

Sourdough takes a lot of practice and experience. Here we will make a sourdough starter, learn how to feed and take care of it. We will also discuss the different ingredients that make up sourdough bread vs. other sourdough things.
