Let's get to Cooking!

Let's be honest, if we didn't have anything to eat we wouldn't make it long. Food is arguably the most important thing to have in your life. Cooking is not really an exact science as baking can be. Cooking is a lot of "a bit of this" "a dash of that", which is why so many people have a hard time with cooking. When you leave each class, you'll have a recipe to share with your friends.

Asian Foods

Asian foods have so much flavor and spices to it, as well as types. This class will go over Chinese food, Japanese food, Thai food, Vitameise food and more!

Italian Foods

Italian food is a very popular type of food. But did you know, Italian food is more than just pasta? We will learn how to make homemade pasta, different types of pasta sauces and more!

Spanish Food!

This class is probably the biggest one we offer. We will learn food from every spanish speaking country. Everything from Mexico, to Argentina, to Puerto Rico and Spain. Here we will learn as much of the cultures as we can, while also making delicious food!

** Note: The food in this class gets spicy, sign up for this class at your discretion.

Camping Foods!

Do you enjoy going camping? Are you tired of having the same basic meals? If you answered yes to both questions, you are in the right place! We will start off indoors and learn how to prep for your outdoor meals. We will then move to a safe, controlled area outdoors where you will learn to start and maintain a fire, as well as how to cook the meals you prepped inside.

** Note: This class is only offered based on the weather.
